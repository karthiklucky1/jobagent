## Delivery tips (say it well)
- **Short sentences.** Breathe between them. Don't rush the hard truths — pause after 💣 lines.
- **Smile on the jokes.** Your face sells the joke more than the words.
- **Show, then say.** Put the thing on screen first, then explain it. People believe what they see.
- **Record in small pieces.** One section per take. Stitch later. Way less stress than one long take.
- **Add captions.** Most people watch on mute. CapCut auto-captions in one click.
