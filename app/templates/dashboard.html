How Boards Crawling Works

HirePath connects directly to Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, SmartRecruiters, and Workday APIs for companies in the registry. Every 6 hours it validates active endpoints and pulls new postings into your pool. Jobs from aggregators (LinkedIn, Indeed, RemoteOK, etc.) are also fetched via job-board APIs and merged with deduplication.
