Welcome to HirePath — here's how to get started

1

Upload Resume

Click 📄 Resume in the sidebar. Upload your PDF — the AI reads your skills, experience and location to match jobs.

2

Paste a Job Link

Use 🔗 Add Job from Link below. Paste any job URL — LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Indeed, Workday. We fetch the JD and calculate your match % + hire probability instantly.

3

Tailored Docs

In 📋 Pipeline, click ✨ Tailor on any shortlisted job. AI rewrites your resume bullets and writes a cover letter matched to that exact job description.

4

Apply

Click 📋 View Tailored Docs to read your resume & cover letter, then 🖥️ Open Form + 📝 Answer Pack to apply. Greenhouse/Lever/Ashby jobs get ⚡ Auto-fill (Pro).

How Board Crawling Works

1

ATS Direct

Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby & Workday APIs are queried directly for companies in your registry — no middleman, freshest data.

2

Aggregators

LinkedIn, Jooble, Reed, RemoteOK & SerpAPI are scraped every 6h and merged with deduplication across all sources.

3

AI Matching

Discovered jobs are scored against your profile via BM25 → FAISS → cross-encoder → Claude LLM, then shortlisted by hire probability.
