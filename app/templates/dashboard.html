ATS Registry Strategy

To avoid scraping generic corporate boards, the Company Registry maintains API hooks to specific company job boards on Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby. During each discovery loop, it validates active endpoints, pulls their job counts, and ingests postings matching AI keywords into our vector database.

We recently added key AI startup boards: Weights & Biases, Fireworks AI, Lightning AI, and Cerebras. These companies hire early-career ML developers, increasing matching throughput.
