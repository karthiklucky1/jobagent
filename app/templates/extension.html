HirePath Extension

Auto-fill job forms in your browser — Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, LinkedIn, Indeed, Workday and more.

⬇️ Download Extension (.zip)

Install in Chrome (3 steps)

1

Unzip the downloaded file

Extract hirepath-extension.zip to a folder on your computer.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

Go to chrome://extensions → enable Developer mode (top right toggle).

3

Load the extension

Click Load unpacked → select the hirepath-extension folder you extracted.

How to use

1️⃣ Open HirePath dashboard and find a job card
2️⃣ Click 🧩 Fill with Extension — the job form opens in a new tab
3️⃣ Click the HirePath icon in your toolbar → ⚡ Fill This Form Now
4️⃣ Review every field — then click Submit yourself
