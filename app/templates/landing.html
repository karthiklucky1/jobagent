How it works

Up and running in minutes

Four steps to a job search that runs itself.

1

Upload your résumé

Drop your PDF or DOCX. AI extracts your skills, experience, target roles, location and work-authorization automatically — no forms.

2

The agent discovers & ranks

HirePath scans 1,000+ boards in your country, scores each role against your résumé, and shortlists only the strong matches — live on your board.

3

AI tailors your application

For each shortlisted role, it generates a tailored résumé + cover letter and pre-fills the application form for you to review.

4

You review & apply

Open a role, see exactly why it matched, and apply in a click. Mark it submitted and track it through to interview — nothing goes out without you.
